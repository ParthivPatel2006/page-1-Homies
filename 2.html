Superset Actively Hiring

India's First University Recruiting Platform

Software Development Engineer I

₹25L - ₹28L • No equity | Bengaluru | 2 years of exp | Full Time

Visa Sponsorship: Not Available

Relocation: Allowed

Remote Work Policy: In office - WFH flexibility

Skills: Java Spring Boot Angular Agile Logical Reasoning Spring Framework

About the job

About the Role

We're looking for top engineering talent to join our small, customer-driven development teams to work on a variety of projects including:

  • Back End: We write lots of microservices, primarily with Java 8. Our APIs are RESTful and use the Spring framework. We take advantage of AWS/EC2, RDS, ElasticSearch for processing volumes of data.
  • Front End: Our web applications are complex, single-page apps written in JavaScript (React, AngularJS, SCSS).
  • Mobile App: Our mobile applications for Android and iOS are built on React Native framework.
  • Core Data, Infrastructure, & Reliability: Building the systems that power hundreds of services on AWS, and empowering access to massive datasets with ElasticSearch, Redis, and MySQL.

At Superset, software is owned and developed by small, autonomous, cross-functional teams. You and your team set the goals, set timelines, build the software, go to user tests, and monitor the performance.

What do we look for?

  • You're a strong coder; regardless of which language you use or how you learned it, we're looking for people who can write clean, effective, and performant code.
  • You have previously worked on a modern web application and have built/integrated with REST APIs along with building front-end using one or more JavaScript frameworks.
  • You are a self-starter and enjoy collaboration with others—our work is carried out in a vacuum, and everyone contributes to get the job done.
  • You understand that making mistakes means you're learning.
  • You have a passion to build great and enduring products.
  • You are passionate about product craftsmanship and care about great design, not only in products and their visual presentation but in your code and technical architecture as well.
  • You are comfortable in a high-paced environment and can respond to urgent requests.

Instead of trying to fill specific slots, we hire talented people at all seniority levels and work with them to find a role that suits them best. Confidence can sometimes hold us back from applying for a job. We'll let you in on a secret: there's no such thing as the perfect candidate. Superset is a place where everyone can grow. So however you identify and whatever background you bring with you, apply if this is a role that would make you excited to come into work every day.

About Superset

Superset is India's Largest University Recruitment Platform - a placement automation tool for universities and a digital campus hiring platform for employers. Our team is dedicated towards leveraging the technology to solve the challenges and problems you could think of during a typical placement season.

We are a Blume Ventures backed Startup (seeded 2022), backed by Great Learning simplifying the graduate recruitment market in India by connecting students and employers via campus placement cells. At present, we work with 400+ colleges and 1200+ recruiters in India and are growing fast. Companies like Cognizant, Capgemini, Hexaware, HDFC Bank, RPG Group and 100's more use Superset to automate their campus hiring process.
